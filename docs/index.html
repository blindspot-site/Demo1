Food Guide

Where to Eat

Two legendary food districts — each a universe of flavour.

🌆 Karol Bagh

A vibrant urban neighbourhood beloved for its all-day food scene — parathas at dawn, chaat at dusk, everything in between.

Famous dishes
Puri Sabzi Aloo Tikki Chole Bhature Jalebi Gol Gappe

🕌 Chandni Chowk

One of Asia's oldest markets — its narrow lanes have fed emperors for 400 years. An unmissable pilgrimage for food lovers.

Famous dishes
Paratha Wali Gali Daulat Ki Chaat Kachori Kulfi Faluda Nihari
